# Can I Upload AncestryDNA to MyHeritage?

Direct upload of AncestryDNA data to MyHeritage is usually not supported.

## Reason
Both platforms use different file formats and structures.

## What happens if you try
- Upload may fail
- Data may be rejected
- Errors in interpretation may occur

## Solution
Convert the file before uploading:
https://tendna.com/en/dna-converter

## Summary
Conversion is required for compatibility between platforms.
